Rainy Days

Rainy Days – An e-commerce website designed as part of my Noroff Cross-Course Project. The brief was to create an online store for a brand selling rain jackets and outerwear. The design focuses on a clean, user-friendly interface, with a modern aesthetic that highlights the products while offering a seamless shopping experience for customers.

Community Science Museum

Community Science Museum – A fun and engaging website designed as part of my Noroff semester project. The goal was to create an interactive platform that captures the attention of children and young teens, from elementary to middle school age. The design focuses on vibrant visuals and playful elements, encouraging exploration and learning about science in an exciting way.

Culinary Creations

An interactive blogging platform for food enthusiasts, created as part of my Noroff exam project. Users can create an account, log in, and share blog posts. The platform allows writing, editing, and deleting posts, while exploring content that blends food with various intriguing themes.
